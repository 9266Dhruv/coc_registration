⚔️ CWL Guidelines

CWL Rules

Everything you need to know about our Lazy CWL format.

📢

Hey guys, we've decided to make some changes in upcoming CWL's!

We're going to do Lazy CWL 🐼

❓

What Does Lazy CWL Mean?

Lazy CWL basically means doing CWL + FWA wars at the same time in 2 different clans. You play CWL in a separate clan while keeping FWA running in the home clan.

📋

Things Every CWL Participant Must Know

  • We continue 50v50 FWA WARS in home clan
  • A new clan will be made for CWL — Clan Link will be sent 2 days before CWL. Bookmark it!
  • Keep 💎 FWA BASE 💎 active throughout the time of CWL
✅

Benefits of Lazy CWL

  • You can do CWL with heroes down
  • You play in a higher league — extra medals!
  • 3x loot from FWA + CWL combined
  • Stress free — just aim for 8 stars from 7 attacks
  • Experiment with all different kinds of attacks you want!
⚠️

Disadvantages of Lazy CWL

  • You have to hop from home clan to CWL clan daily during CWL week
  • We lose CWL rounds — but we benefit with more CWL medals in the end
  • Opponents will be TH14/13 players — just get 8 stars from 7 attacks
🔗

Important Notes

Clan Links will be sent 48–36 hours prior to CWL beginning, along with a clan mail notification. Make sure you check announcements regularly!

🎯

Star Target

Your goal is simple — achieve a minimum of 8 stars from 7 attacks across the CWL week. No pressure, just do your best and have fun experimenting!
